Logarithm of the sum of exponentiations of the inputs.

@description
Calculates `log(exp(x1) + exp(x2))`.

@returns
Output tensor, element-wise logarithm of the sum of exponentiations
of the inputs.

@param x1
Input tensor.

@param x2
Input tensor.
