Compute the outer product of two vectors.

@description
Given two vectors `x1` and `x2`, the outer product is:

```
out[i, j] = x1[i] * x2[j]
```

@returns
    Outer product of `x1` and `x2`.

@param x1
First input tensor.

@param x2
Second input tensor.
